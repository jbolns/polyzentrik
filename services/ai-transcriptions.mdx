For cost and privacy reasons, sometimes there is a need to keep data "close to heart" and perform AI transcriptions locally on your own equipment/infrastructure.

We can help you do this.
* **Open source resources.** We have published [open-source AI transcription resources](../../resources/local-transcriptions) that can help your implement your own solution.
* **Products.** We also offer [LOKAL transcriptions](../../products/lokal-transcriptions/), an App you can easily install and use on your own computer.
* **Services.** We can also help you implement AI transcriptions as part of your own infrastructure/system – check some of the available options below and [**get in touch**](mailto:hello@polyzentrik.com) to discuss possibilities!
