Having a website that is, all, fast, beautiful, and sustainable can help you:
* **Improve user experience (UX).** Loading speeds are nowadays critical to retaining users on your website.
* **Increase conversion rates.** A seamless navigation experience can contribute to longer visits and higher conversion rates.
* **Solidify your competitive advantage.** A fast and well-designed website showcases your commitment to excellence.
* **Improve SEO rankings.** Fast and efficient load times contribute to SEO and therefore increase your site's visibility.
* **Increase brand reputation.** Users across the planet increasingly value a company's commitment to sustainability.
* **Save money, a lot of it.** Sustainable websites can reduce energy consumption, hosting bills, and the need for carbon offsets.

It is that simple. Better for the wallet. Better for the planet.

When it comes to website design, business success and environmental responsibility go hand in hand.

### Our open-source resources
We have open-source resources that can help you develop more sustainable websites.
* **[WEIRD](../../resources/weird/).** A multimodal web-framework that can be used to produce websites from little more than just documents (including Word documents). The websites produced by WEIRD can be highly sustainable.
